About Mama

The siren Instructor

Mama Lavish is a spiritual hoodoo queen from the south side of Chicago. She founded The Siren Sage against bigoted and harmful spiritual communities. Also known as The Siren Botanica, her store is a safe space for spicy workers, spicy rootworkers and witches. Mama Lavish wishes for people to navigate their spiritual journey without judgement or harsh criticism like most do on social media. Lavish believes that experiencing spiritual and physical freedom is extremely important. Join her as she travels on her journey educating others and helping keep the spiritual community less harsh.
