Jadyn Wilson

From a young age she gravitated towards anything creative until her first film class her sophomore year of high school changing her life. Loving the role of director making her final film for that class making a small film having a fun summer day with friends.

Right now she’s inspired by seeing black stories made by black filmmakers.

amazed by the sinners movie seeing a dark part of our past turned into an amazing horror movie with an amazing plus size dark skin main love interest these type of films remind her to remain focused on her path and her journey.
